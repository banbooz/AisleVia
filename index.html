This proof of concept needs a WebXR-capable browser, an AR-capable phone, and an HTTPS address. It is designed for Chrome on an ARCore-supported Android phone.

AisleVia ARROOM POC V2

Align the digital twin to the real room

This version uses two real-world points instead of guessing the room direction from the phone alone.

  1. Place the first point on the doorway floor.
  2. Place the second point on the carpet directly in front of the fireplace centre.
  3. Select an item. If its photo-estimated location is wrong, tap Teach exact item spot once.
The room map and learned item coordinates stay in this browser. Camera frames are not uploaded by this prototype.
