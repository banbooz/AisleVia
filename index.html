AisleVia ARROOM POC

Real arrows on your real floor

Use the living room as a pretend shop. The phone tracks your movement, lays a route over the carpet, points up toward the target, and places a pulsing red highlight over the item.

  1. Stand in the doorway and face the fireplace.
  2. Start AR, slowly scan the carpet, and aim the green circle at the doorway floor.
  3. Tap Place entrance, then choose an item.
Camera frames stay on the phone. This prototype does not upload or save them.
